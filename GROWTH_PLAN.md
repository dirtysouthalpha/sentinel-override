## Phase 3: Growth (500-2000 stars)

### Feature Differentiation
What makes Sentinel Override *different* from Claude for Chrome, Browser Use, etc.:

| Feature | Sentinel Override | Claude for Chrome | Browser Use |
|---------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Cost Safeguards** | ✅ Hard-coded, cannot bypass | ❌ No limits | ❌ No limits |
| **Open Source** | ✅ MIT License | ❌ Closed | ✅ Apache |
| **Multi-Provider** | ✅ OpenRouter, Venice, z.ai | ❌ Claude only | ❌ Limited |
| **Plan-Decompose** | ✅ Show + Approve + Execute | ✅ Similar | ❌ Direct execution |
| **Local TTS** | ✅ JennyNeural on port 8765 | ❌ No | ❌ No |
